Crisp Marinated Cucumbers

This is my grandma's recipe for marinated cucumbers — I absolutely love it. It's great eating it during the warmer months or at any family get-together.

Ingredients

  • ½ cup white vinegar
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon celery seed
  • 2 medium cucumbers, sliced
  • ¼ cup sliced sweet onion

Steps

  1. Whisk vinegar, sugar, salt, and celery seed together in a large bow. Add cucumbers and onion and stir until well coated.
  2. Cover and refrigerate 8 hours to overnight. Serve cold.
